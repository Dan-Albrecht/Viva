# Recommended rollout plan for Viva Glint feedback programs

Use the following tables to build a rollout plan for your Viva Glint programs. 

> [!NOTE] 
> The following plans assume a six-week launch, which may be longer than the timeline you anticipate.  If your timeline is shorter, compress or thin the communications to align appropriately.

## Pre-survey rollout plan

|**Timing before survey start**| **Message to your organization**|**Key activities**|**Owner**|**Communication template links**|
|-----------|-----------|----------------|--------|-------------------------------|
|6 weeks prior|<ul><li>Establish program overview</li><li>Define roles and responsibilities</li></ul>|<ul><li>Review best practices and develop rollout plan with HR and communications team </li><li> Schedule company-level results reviews </li><li> Schedule business until results review</li></ul>|Project team, HR|[Prelaunch email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|
|5 weeks prior|<ul><li>Vision and strategy planning </li><li>Inform about resources </li><li> Discuss importance of customizing program|<ul><li>Develop communications plan and messaging </li><li> Customize platform messages </li><li> Finalize rollout plan </li><li> Prepare Vision and Strategy materials</li></ul>|Project team|[Prelaunch email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|
|4 weeks prior|<ul><li>Provide engagement program overview and relationship to business success using Viva Glint </li><li> Provide role expectations and timing, HR and manager resources, and training signups|<ul><li>Customize results rollout plan </li><li> Sign up for platform training</li></ul>|HR Exec, HRBPs|[HRBP prep email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|
|3 weeks prior|<ul><li>Continue leadership communications and training signups|<ul><li>Refine plan to share company-wide results with execs </li><li> Support HRBPs for rollout planning </li><li> Finalize rollout planning </li><li> Prepare execs to champion Viva Glint program|HR Exec, Project team, HRBPs|[Leadership prep email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|
|2 weeks prior|<ul><li>Send communications for HRBPs training</li><li>Reinforce role expectations and timing</li><li>Send communications for manager resources and training signup</li></ul>|<ul><li>Ensure platform trainings and resources are being used</li><li>If customer communications are being used, prepare to send and host them on the intranet</li><li>Distribute printer flyers, if using</li></ul>|Project team, HRBPs|[HRBP prep email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|
|1 week prior|<ul><li>Emphasize manager communications</li></ul>|<ul><li>Encourage manager training signup</li><li>Prepare to report on response rates</li></ul>|Project team, HRBPs|[Prelaunch email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|
|1 day prior|<ul><li>Final all-organization communication|<ul><li>Build excitement!|CEO/Senior Exec| [Prelaunch email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2241178)|

### Post-survey close rollout plan

|**Timing after survey closes**| **Message to your organization**|**Key activities**|**Owner**|**Communication template links**|
|-----------|-----------|----------------|--------|-------------------------------|
|1 day after close|<ul><li>Company-wide thank you emails, response timing emails, and next steps information</li><li>Release results to leaders with phased access</li></ul>|<ul><li>Platform review</li></ul>|HRBPs|[Company-wide survey close email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)|
|1-2 days after close|<ul><li>Senior leader/managers results available communications</li><li> Next steps communications</li></ul>|<ul><li>Share results with execs</li><li>Discuss focus areas and begin to formulate feedback action plan </li><li> Review results in platform using guides and training</li></ul>| Project team, Senior leaders, HRBPs, managers|[Senior leader and manager email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)|   
|3-4 days after close|<ul><li>Company-wide result communications</li><li> Encourage employee involvement in action planning and shared accountability</li></ul>|<ul><li>Finalize company-wide focus areas</li><li>Encourage opportunities for change</li></ul>|All level employees|[Company-wide email template](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)|   
|5-7 days after close|<ul><li>Manager communications</li></ul>|<ul><li>Share results with execs</li><li>Support company-wide action plan ownership with ideas, resources, and communication plans </li><li> Share best practices </li><li>Support manager on feedback sessions and communicating action plans </li><li>Role model best practices, discuss action plans and progress at staff meetings and maintain accountability </li><li>Use tools and resources to create action plans </li><li>Maintain supportive contact with managers</li></ul>|Project team, Senior leaders, HRBPs, Managers|[Manager results email templates](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)|   
|3-12 weeks after close|<ul><li>HRBP communications</li></ul>|<ul><li>Use tools and resources to take action</li><li>Update action progress in Viva Glint platform </li><li> Support company-wide initiatives</li></ul>|Project team, Managers|[HRBP email template](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)| 
|4-8 weeks after close|<ul><li>HRBP communications</li></ul>|<ul><li>Use tools and resources to take action</li><li>Update action progress in Viva Glint platform </li><li> Support company-wide initiatives</li></ul>|Project team, Managers|[HRBP email template](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)| 
|10 weeks after close|<ul><li>HRBP communications</li></ul>|<ul><li>Use tools and resources to take action</li><li>Update action progress in Viva Glint platform </li><li> Support company-wide initiatives</li></ul>|Project team, Managers|[HRBP email template](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2231018)|
